Understanding the Politics

A detailed take on the politics behind the numbers and the rhetoric

Under Trump, negotiations have been less about data, policy and a calculated responses and more about politics, personal relationships and the associated unpredictability. This segment deals with all the key political tools, events and crisis that shaped these negotiations and deals with issues that are beyond data, trade and economics that have impacted the outcomes.
